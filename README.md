# Emotion-AwarePainMeasurementSystem
## ProblemStatement
Pain assessment in healthcare is currently subjective and unreliable, as it depends on patients self-reporting their pain on a scale (typically 1–10). This approach often leads to:


Inconsistent and inaccurate pain evaluation


Difficulty in assessmentfor non-verbal patients (children, elderly, unconscious)


Delays in diagnosis and treatment




Lack of continuous and real-time monitoring



As a result, healthcare providers lack a standardized, objective method to accurately measure and track patient pain.
